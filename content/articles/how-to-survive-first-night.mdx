Your first night is the most dangerous moment in any new world.

## First Five Minutes

Collect 16 logs. Craft a crafting table, wooden tools, then stone tools as fast as possible.

## Shelter

Dig into a hill or build a small enclosed structure. Block every entrance before dark.

## Light

Place torches everywhere inside and around your shelter. Mobs spawn in darkness.

## Bed

Kill three sheep and craft a bed to skip the night entirely.

## Final Thoughts

Collect wood, build fast, place torches, sleep if you can.

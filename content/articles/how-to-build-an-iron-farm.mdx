Building an iron farm is one of the best investments you can make.

## How Iron Farms Work

Villagers frightened by a zombie spawn iron golems. The farm kills them and collects the iron automatically.

## What You Need

- Three beds and three villagers
- One zombie
- Lava to kill golems
- Hoppers and chests

## Building Step by Step

Build a platform five blocks high. Add beds and villagers. Place a zombie below in a minecart. Build a lava kill chamber below with hoppers collecting drops.

## Final Thoughts

A basic farm produces 30 to 40 iron ingots per hour. Build it early and let it run.

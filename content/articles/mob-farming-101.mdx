A mob farm is one of the first farms every player should build.

## Spawner Farms

Find a dungeon spawner. Add water channels funneling mobs into a 22 block drop shaft. Hoppers collect drops at the bottom.

## Dark Room Farms

Build a large dark platform above Y 128. Mobs spawn only on your platform and fall into the collection point.

## What You Get

Zombie farms give iron and XP. Skeleton farms give bones and arrows.

## Final Thoughts

Build a spawner farm as soon as you find a dungeon.

Diamonds define your Minecraft progression. Here is how to find them efficiently.

## The Correct Y Level

Mine at Y negative 58. This is the highest diamond concentration in 1.21.

## Branch Mining

Dig a main tunnel at Y negative 58 and branch off every two blocks. Maximum coverage with minimum wasted effort.

## Fortune III

Always mine diamonds with Fortune III. Each ore can drop up to four diamonds.

## Lava Safety

Always carry a water bucket. Never mine a block without seeing what is behind it.

## Final Thoughts

Mine at Y negative 58, branch mine, and always use Fortune III.

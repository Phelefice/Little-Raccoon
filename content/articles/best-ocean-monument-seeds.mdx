Ocean monuments are some of the most rewarding structures in Minecraft — massive prismarine fortresses hiding treasure, rare sponge rooms, and blocks of gold. But finding one can take a long time if you're just wandering the seas. These seeds put ocean monuments close to your spawn, so you can get right into the action. Whether you're playing Java Edition or Bedrock, there's something here for you.

## Why Farm Ocean Monuments?

Before diving into the seeds, let's talk about why ocean monuments are worth your time:

- **8 Gold Blocks:** Hidden in the core of every monument — worth 72 gold ingots total.
- **Sponge:** Found in sponge rooms, sponge is only obtainable here (besides creative mode). Essential for draining water areas.
- **Prismarine & Sea Lanterns:** Beautiful building materials with a unique aquatic aesthetic.
- **Guardians:** Killing them drops prismarine shards and crystals, plus a decent amount of XP.
- **Elder Guardians:** Three per monument — defeating them gives you Mining Fatigue resistance… wait, they *give* you fatigue. Bring milk!

Ready to get wet? Here are the best seeds.

## Tips for Conquering Ocean Monuments

### Gear Up Before You Go

- **Respiration III helmet** — Massively extends breath underwater
- **Aqua Affinity** — Lets you mine at full speed while submerged
- **Depth Strider III** — Move fast underwater like you're on land
- **Turtle Shell helmet** — Grants Water Breathing for 10 seconds when at low breath

### Bring the Right Potions

- **Water Breathing (8 min)** — Mandatory for deep dives
- **Night Vision** — Monuments are dark and poorly lit
- **Strength II** — Elder Guardians have a lot of health

### Deal with Mining Fatigue

Elder Guardians inflict Mining Fatigue III the moment you get near the monument — this makes breaking blocks nearly impossible. Counter it by:

- Drinking **Milk** to instantly clear the effect
- Using **TNT** for explosive block removal (careful with water physics)
- Pre-placing TNT-filled holes before engaging Elder Guardians

### Drain the Monument

For the easiest possible looting experience, consider draining the water from the monument entirely. Use sponges (from inside the monument itself) or sand walls to enclose sections. It's a big project, but it turns the monument into a regular dungeon.

## What to Do After the Monument

Once you've cleared all three Elder Guardians and grabbed the gold blocks, the monument itself becomes a great base. Here's what players often turn them into:

- **Guardian farms** — Spawners produce guardians endlessly. Build a farm for prismarine, XP, and fish.
- **Underwater base** — The prismarine structure is already impressive. Add glass domes, interior lighting, and rooms.
- **Aquarium build** — Fill surrounding areas with fish, coral, and sea life for a stunning ocean exhibit.

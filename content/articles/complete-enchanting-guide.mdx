Enchanting is one of the most powerful systems in survival Minecraft.

## Setup

Place 15 bookshelves one block away from your enchanting table for maximum power.

## Best Enchantments

Sword: Sharpness V and Looting III. Pickaxe: Fortune III and Efficiency V. Armor: Protection IV everywhere and Feather Falling IV on boots. Mending on everything.

## Getting Specific Enchantments

Trade with librarian villagers. Cure zombie villagers for permanent discounts.

## XP Farm

Build a mob spawner or enderman farm for endless experience.

## Final Thoughts

Prioritize Mending early. Fully enchanted gear transforms survival completely.

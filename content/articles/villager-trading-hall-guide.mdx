A trading hall is one of the most powerful systems in survival Minecraft.

## Best Professions

Librarian for Mending and enchantment books. Farmer for unlimited emeralds. Armorer for discounted diamond gear.

## Building the Hall

Give each villager one bed and one job site block. Separate each pair. Enclose the hall to prevent zombie attacks.

## Curing Zombie Villagers

Use a splash potion of weakness and a golden apple. Cured librarians sell Mending books for one emerald.

## Final Thoughts

Start with librarians and farmers. Cure every villager for maximum value.

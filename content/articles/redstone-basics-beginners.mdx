Redstone is Minecraft electricity. Even basic knowledge unlocks powerful automation.

## Power Sources

Levers toggle on and off. Buttons send a brief pulse. Pressure plates activate on contact. Redstone torches emit constant signals.

## Dust and Repeaters

Dust carries signals up to 15 blocks. Repeaters extend signals further and add timing delay.

## First Builds

Automatic door using pistons and a pressure plate. Hidden staircase with sticky pistons and a lever.

## Final Thoughts

Start with an automatic door. Every complex machine is just simple components combined.
